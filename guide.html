How to Play Android Flappy

Welcome to Android Flappy! The objective of the game is to navigate a bird through a series of pipes. The bird flaps upwards when you click anywhere on the screen. The goal is to avoid colliding with the pipes and score points by passing through them.

Controls:

- Click anywhere on the screen to make the bird flap and rise.

- Try to keep the bird flying and navigate through the gaps in the pipes.

Good luck and have fun!
